CAFÉ TIPS

Useful tips for a better café experience ☕

1. Visit During Off-Peak Hours

Visit cafés during weekdays or non-peak hours to enjoy a more relaxing atmosphere and avoid crowds.

2. Find the Best Photo Spots

Look for natural lighting areas such as windows or outdoor seating for better aesthetic photos.

3. Try the Signature Menu

Every café has unique signature dishes or drinks. Don’t miss the opportunity to try them.

4. Check Social Media Before Visiting

Follow cafés on social media to stay updated on promotions, menus, and events.

5. Be Respectful and Patient

Be patient during peak hours and respect the café environment and staff.

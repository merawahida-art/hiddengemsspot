About Rumah Kopi Purnama

Rumah Kopi Purnama is a hidden gem nestled in Sungai Kantan, Kajang, offering a peaceful and relaxing environment for coffee enthusiasts. Surrounded by greenery and designed with a rustic village-inspired concept, the café provides a cozy atmosphere that is perfect for spending quality time with family and friends. Visitors can enjoy handcrafted coffee, light meals, and desserts while experiencing the calm ambience, especially during the evening. Its charming outdoor setting and warm hospitality make Rumah Kopi Purnama an ideal destination for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life.
